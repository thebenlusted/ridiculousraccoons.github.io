About This Project

Ridiculous Raccoon's is an NFT project crafted entirely by hand. With a team of animal-lovers, our project is in full support of the BC Wildlife Park in Kamloops, British Columbia. All Raccoons are based on real people, characters from movies and video games, and more!

If you'd like to support the Ridiculous Raccoon project further, check out our Discord! Also, follow us on Twitter @kurseddesign
